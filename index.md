# REMOTE DNA — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026

REMOTE DNA ("the App") is a universal infrared (IR) remote-control application
published by **beinteck** (Mohamed Khocha).

## Data we collect

**No personal data.** The App does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or
share any personal information.

- All app data (your saved devices, button mappings, language and theme
  preferences) is stored **locally on your phone only** and never leaves it.
- All remote-control features work fully **offline**.
- The App contains **no advertising SDKs and no behavioural trackers**.
- The App does not access your contacts, location, microphone, files, or any
  other sensitive resource. The camera is used only for the optional QR
  device-sharing feature described below.

### Crash reports

To keep the App stable, anonymous **crash reports** are sent to Sentry
(sentry.io, EU data region) **only if the App crashes**. A report contains the
technical error, app version, and device model/OS version. It contains **no
personal data, no identifiers you entered, and no saved devices**, and it is
never used for advertising. You can block this at any time by denying the App
network access — every remote-control feature keeps working.

## Permissions

- `android.permission.TRANSMIT_IR` — required to emit infrared signals through
  your phone's IR blaster so the App can act as a remote control. IR signals
  are one-way light pulses; they carry no personal information.
- `android.permission.CAMERA` — used **only** when you choose
  "Import via QR" to scan a device-sharing QR code shown on another phone.
  Camera frames are processed on your device in real time; **no photo or video
  is ever captured, stored, or uploaded**. You can deny this permission and
  every other feature keeps working.

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. You can also clear the
App's data at any time from Android Settings.

## Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone,
including children.

## Changes

If a future version ever changes these practices (for example, optional cloud
features), this policy will be updated **before** that version ships and the
in-app link will point to the revised text.
